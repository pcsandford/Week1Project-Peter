A Little About Me

My name is Peter Cameron and I am a freelance web-developer and designer originally from Scotland and now based in Toronto. I have experience with HTML, CCS, Sass, JavaScript, and JQuery among others. I love coding, new challenges, and working with people to bring their designs to life. I am also interested in game design, and try to incorporate interactive elements into my web-development projects. Please take a look at my portfolio for more examples of my work. Please also check out my blog updates on my other interests including street art and vinyl toys.

My Current Projects

After being inspired by the video games I spent hours playing as a child, I have designed and built various games for iPhone, iPad and Android devices. In addition to working on more traditional website design, I am also currently working on designs that use this unique asthetic in new and original ways, creating dynamic and responsive experiences which put a modern spin on classic video game art and effects.
